About Gopal Farms

We are affiliated to Gopal Farm LLC (USA), with farms in various states of the USA, that practice Regenerative Agriculture. The proven model of Gopal Farm owns and serves on 1000+ acres of farmland through their 4 successful farms and more than 5 farm projects in the pipeline.

Gopal Farms - Cape Cod

Gopal Farm Cape Cod is a 223-acre Ag-conserved farm focused on regenerative practices, the ethical treatment of animals, and supporting the local community with fresh produce.

Gopal Farms - Sprout Creek

Gopal Farm Sprout creek is located at " 34 Lauer Rd, Poughkeepsie, NY 12603, United States ".

Our Services

Things that set Project GAIYA apart from others

Best Breed Selection

Our focus will be on keeping the indigenous breeds at our shelter pure to ensure higher milk production and better adaptation to Indian climate.

Veterinary Care

Our cow sanctuary will ensure lifetime veterinarian care to stray or abondoned cows. We'll make sure they also get proper fodder and roaming space.

Cow Dung Plastic & Fabric

We have the roadmap to setup research and production units to produce and supply cow dung based plastic and fabric at a commercial scale.
